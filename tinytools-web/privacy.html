Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Browser-based tools

TinyTools is designed so common tools such as word counting, image resizing, password generation, QR creation, and random picking run in your browser. The site does not require an account and does not intentionally upload your text, images, generated passwords, or lists for these tools.

Advertising and third-party services

TinyTools may use Google AdSense after the site is approved for ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to provide, measure, and improve ads. You can review Google's advertising privacy information and browser controls through Google's own privacy resources.

Local storage

The site may store small preferences in your browser, such as language or install-related settings, so the interface is easier to use the next time you visit.

Contact

If you need to report a privacy concern, contact the site owner through the platform where this site is hosted.
