Privacy Policy

Random Tool Studio provides simple browser-based random draw tools. We do not require account registration and we do not intentionally collect personal information through these tools.

Information processed in your browser

Text entered into the ladder, wheel, and number draw tools is used only to run the tool in your browser. The site does not operate a custom server database for storing those entries.

Advertising

This site may use Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. You can learn about Google's advertising data practices at Google's advertising and privacy resources.

Local storage

The site may save your selected language in local storage so the interface can open in the same language next time.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the site owner through the platform or profile where this site is published.
