Privacy Policy

Random Tool Studio provides browser-based random draw tools for ladder games, spin wheels, and number draws. We do not require account registration and we do not intentionally collect personal information through these tools.

Information processed in your browser

Names, results, wheel items, excluded numbers, and number ranges entered into the tools are used to run the selected draw in your browser. The site does not operate a custom server database for storing those entries.

Advertising

This site may use Google AdSense after approval. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure ad performance, personalize ads where allowed, and prevent fraud. You can learn about Google's advertising data practices through Google's advertising and privacy resources.

Local storage

The site may save your selected language in local storage so the interface can open in the same language next time.

Children and sensitive information

Random Tool Studio is designed as a general utility site. Please do not enter sensitive personal information, passwords, official identification numbers, or private contact details into the draw fields.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the site owner through the platform or profile where this site is published.
